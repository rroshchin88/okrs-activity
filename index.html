Objective

Tip: An effective objective should be inspirational, actionable, and free of any metrics. For example, "Inspire our team to achieve top customer satisfaction scores."

Key Results

Tip: Effective key results should be measurable and time-bound. For example, "Increase customer satisfaction from 70% to 90% by the end of Q4."
